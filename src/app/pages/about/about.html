Our Story

Where it began

We grew up in Guntur, surrounded by fields that fed us before they ever fed a business.

Long before MR Organics had a name, we knew the smell of drying chilli on the wind and the colour of moringa freshly ground. Agriculture was not something we studied — it was the ground under our feet, the rhythm of every season.

When we moved to the city, the food changed. Powders were bulked out, colours were added, and prices climbed for things that no longer tasted of the plant. So we went back to the farms we grew up around and started buying direct — small, honest, and unadulterated — to bring those authentic superfoods into modern kitchens across India.
